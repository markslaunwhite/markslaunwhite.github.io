About Me

Hi there! Thanks for swinging by.

I'm currently a student in the Sobey School of Business in Halifax studying Marketing and Computing Science. I'm interested in film, photography, growth hacking and anything involving tech or startups.

Over the past two years, I have been very involved with various societies while attending Saint Mary's University such as the Enactus society as well as Habitat for Humanity. I also spent a lot of time working with the students' society as a board member. I also co-founded a marketing business in 2013 while an underdergraduate student.

I'm a lover of coffee, Toronto sports, and re-runs of "How I Met Your Mother" on Netflix. If you like anything revolving around tech, social media, entrepreneurship, sports, travel, or startups, check out my blog with new entries posted every Monday and Thursday!
